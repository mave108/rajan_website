Sajan Engineering Works Pvt. Ltd.

With a growing demand of engineering equipment in construction industry, Sajan Engineering Works Pvt. Ltd. is engaged in developing paver block molds and other such solutions. With a sophisticated infrastructural base, we are offering Hydraulic Tile Press, Vibration Table, Paver Block Steel Mold, Paver Block Steel Die, Paver Block Mold, Paver Steel Mold, etc. Used to form paver blocks, etc. to create pavements for automobile parking and pathways; our products are appreciated for possessing required features including high quality uniform pressing, long service life, etc. We have hired specialists to fabricate these molds with highest quality standards.
